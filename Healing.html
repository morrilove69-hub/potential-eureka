Begin by Sitting Alone

We truly do not know ourselves until we've spent time with ourselves first.

Start by trying an activity you loved as a child similar to painting or building blocks.

Ask yourself, "Do I still enjoy this? What do I enjoy in it? What could make this a better experience?"

Now try a healthy activity you did as a teen

ask, "Do I like this? Do I still have a good time doing this?"

Take your time and ruminate on these questions.

Truly, are you still having fun doing these activities? Is this still fun?

Now that you've connected with your inner child and your inner teen; how do you feel?

What are some other activities that relate to the great ones I've experienced?

How can I integrate them into my daily life?
